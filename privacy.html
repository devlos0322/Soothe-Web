1. Data We Process

Local usage data

  • Plans, records, categories, and settings created while using the app
  • Stored on your device by default

When Google integration is enabled

  • Google account info (account ID, email, name)
  • Google Drive backup data
  • Google Calendar sync data (event title, time, description)

2. Why We Use Data

  • Provide core app features (planning, tracking, insights)
  • Maintain account connection and authentication status
  • Handle backup/restore and calendar sync

3. Retention and Deletion

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it
  • Google-integrated data follows Google service policies
  • Related local credentials are removed when you log out

4. Third-Party Sharing and Tracking

  • No sale of personal data for advertising
  • Data may be sent to Google OAuth/Drive/Calendar only for requested features
  • No tracking and no personalized advertising data collection

5. Your Rights and Contact

  • You can delete app data inside the app
  • You can disconnect Google integration by logging out
