Before you contact us

A quick 3-step check helps us solve your issue much faster.

Step 1

Confirm app and device version

Please check your app version, device model, and iOS version first.

Step 2

Capture what happened

Take one screenshot and write short reproduction steps with time information.

Step 3

Send a focused message

Include only key facts and expected result so we can respond quickly.

Include in your message

  • App version
  • Device model / iOS version
  • Time when issue happened
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Screenshot if possible

FAQ

Can I use the app without Google login?

No. At this time, Google login is required to start using the app.

Where are backups stored?

With Google Drive integration, backups are stored in appDataFolder.

Is calendar sync automatic?

Events are created or deleted based on your actions. No automatic rearrangement is performed.
